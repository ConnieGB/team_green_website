Be inspired!

Here's some examples of famous people who are vegetarian and vegan. If they can do it, so can you!

Vegetarians

Drake

Ever wondered what powered Drake's slick dance moves on Hotline Bling? Well it wasn't meat, that's for sure. As of March 2018, Drake does not eat meat. And it's rumoured he's also gone vegan after this Instagram post.

Peter Dinklage

He plays one of the best characters in Game of Thrones (I think we're all rooting for him deep down), and he also does not eat meat! Peter has stated that he has been vegetarian since the age of 16. He is a strong advocate for animal rights and has previously worked with PETA to advocate a vegan diet. Yay Peter!

Leonardo DiCaprio

I don't think there is anything this man can do wrong. It is well known that Leo is passionate about fighting climate change (which is surprising as you would think he wouldn't be a fan of icebergs...), and part of that is his diet. Leo is vegetarian, and is even rumoured to be vegan, although this is unconfirmed. Either way, we are glad he is doing his bit for the environment!

Vegans

Sara Pascoe

She's hilarious, she's intelligent, she's gorgeous - is there anything this woman doesn't have? Well I can tell you one thing - she won't have any cow's milk in her fridge. Sara is an animal lover and follows a vegan diet. She says on her website "Everytime you purchase a vegan product or eat in a more ethical way, you are voting with your money and creating a market that encourages more food makers to utilise compassionate or non-animal farming." How's that for motivation?

Benedict Cumberbatch

It's the actor everyone knows, and yet no one is sure exactly what his surname is. As well as being incredibly talented, Benedict has also spoken about being vegan. He has said that he is 'as much as a vegan as he can be', but it isn't always easy with all the travelling he has to do. Surely if Sherlock Holmes and Dr Strange are vegan, it must be a clever thing to do?

Venus and Serena Williams

They have dominated the tennis world for years. Last year, Serena won the US open whilst pregnant with her first child. They are arguably some of the best tennis players in history, and they are also both vegan! In 2011, Venus turned to a vegan diet for health reasons, and not long after, Serena decided to join her, although they have both stated they do have cheat days from time to time. It doesn't seem to have done any harm to their tennis skills - they are both still dominating the tennis world.
